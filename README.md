# Bright Motors – Car Sales Analysis

# Problem Statement

Bright Motors needs data‑driven insights to support a new Head of Sales in improving sales performance, optimizing inventory, and expanding the dealership network.

# Objective
To analyze historical car sales data and uncover trends in revenue, pricing, customer preferences, and regional performance.

# Aim
Identify high‑performing car makes and models
Understand pricing and purchase trends
Improve profitability and sales efficiency
Clean and transform car sales data
Analyze sales, revenue, and regional trends
Visualize key insights
Present recommendations to stakeholders
